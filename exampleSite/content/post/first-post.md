Paper Reader is designed for articles, notes, and essays that should feel easy to read.

The theme uses system fonts, a narrow measure, generous line height, and a small set of colors. It avoids JavaScript and build-time asset processing so the site remains easy to maintain.

## A Section Heading

Long-form writing benefits from restraint. Navigation stays visible, metadata is quiet, and article content remains the center of the page.

> A good reading theme should disappear while the words remain clear.

You can customize the theme by overriding templates or replacing `static/css/style.css` in your own Hugo site.
